About Us

We publish high-quality monographs, edited volumes, and translated works across fields such as political science, sociology, international relations, development studies, political economy, history, and related disciplines.

Our editorial vision prioritizes intellectual depth, methodological diversity, and a strong commitment to global and comparative perspectives—particularly those emerging from regions and voices often underrepresented in mainstream academic publishing.

Why Publish with Minerva Academic?

Minerva Academic is an independent scholarly publisher based in London, dedicated to advancing critical research and global dialogue in the social sciences. Our mission is to bring forward innovative, rigorous, and impactful scholarship that challenges conventional perspectives and contributes meaningfully to contemporary academic debates.

At Minerva Academic, we believe that scholarly communication should be accessible, collaborative, and internationally oriented. We work closely with authors, editors, and translators at every stage of the publishing process, from proposal development to manuscript preparation, peer review, production, and dissemination.

• Scholarly quality
We are committed to rigorous peer review, careful editorial oversight, and high production standards to ensure that each publication meets the highest academic expectations.

• Author-centred approach
We provide close editorial support from proposal to publication, emphasizing transparent communication, collaborative development of ideas, and respect for the author’s intellectual
vision.

• Global reach
Our books are distributed to academic libraries, universities, research institutions, and specialist readerships worldwide, ensuring that your scholarship reaches the audiences it deserves.
